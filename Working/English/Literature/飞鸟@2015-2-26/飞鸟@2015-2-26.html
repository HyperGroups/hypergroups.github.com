StrayBirds

Stray birds of summer come to my window to sing and fly alway. And yellow leaves of autumn, which have no songs flutter and fall there with a sigh.

O Troupe of little vagrants of the world, leave your footprints in my words.

The world puts off its vastness to its lover. It becomes small as one song, as one kiss of the eternal.

It is the tears of the earth that make her smiles in bloom.

The mighty desert is burning for the love a blade of grass who shakes her head and laughs and flies alway.
